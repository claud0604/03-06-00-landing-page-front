Face Photo Guide

Follow these tips for accurate skin tone and face shape analysis

Front-facing photo only. The AI analyzes skin color, face proportions, and contrast from your front view.

Lighting

  • Natural daylight is best
  • Avoid strong shadows on face
  • No colored lighting (LED, neon)

Appearance

  • Bare face (no makeup)
  • Hair pulled back from face
  • Neutral expression, eyes forward

Body Photo Guide

Follow these tips for accurate body type analysis

Full body front photo. The AI measures shoulder and hip width ratio to determine your body type.

Clothing

  • Wear form-fitting clothes
  • Avoid oversized or layered outfits
  • Solid colors preferred

Posture

  • Stand straight, arms relaxed
  • Face the camera directly
  • Full body visible (head to feet)
